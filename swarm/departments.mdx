The sections below give you a complete directory of every agent, what it does, and what it brings to the table.

<Note>
  You never select specialists manually. The Manager (CEO) and Digital Corporation (COO) automatically choose and coordinate the right agents based on your business objective.
</Note>

## How Specialists Are Selected and Loaded

You never need to specify which agent to use. When you pass a business objective to `zilmate.manager()` or `zilmate swarm`, the COO analyzes the goal and automatically selects the most relevant specialists. Agents are instantiated only when needed and torn down after their sub-tasks complete, keeping token costs minimal.

Specialists are loaded dynamically at runtime — only the agents required for your specific objective are instantiated. This keeps token usage lean and execution fast, regardless of how many total specialists exist in the swarm.
